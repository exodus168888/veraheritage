Our Story

The world wants ube.
The Philippines owns it.

Vera Heritage was founded on a simple truth: Philippine ube is the world's finest — yet Filipino farmers capture less than 4% of its global export value. While demand soars globally, foreign intermediaries fill the gap with imitations.

We are building the infrastructure to change that. Direct farmgate sourcing, transparent supply chains, AI-powered traceability, and equitable profit sharing — putting economic value back where it belongs: with Filipino farmers.

Farmer-First Economics

Why Philippine Ube

The original reigns
supreme.

Endemic to the Philippines, cultivated for centuries. No substitute comes close.

Golden Seal Certified
🌺
Unmatched Aroma & Deep Violet Hue
The Kinampay variety from Bohol has an aromatic profile and deep violet pigmentation impossible to replicate with sweet potato substitutes used by foreign competitors.
🌋
Ideal Terroir — Volcanic Soils, Tropical Climate
Bohol's unique soil composition and concentrated tropical climate produce a flavor intensity found nowhere else. A true product of terroir, like Champagne.
🏛️
Centuries of Cultural Integration
Ube is a cultural icon woven into Filipino life — fiestas, halaya, celebrations. That authenticity is embedded in every harvest, not manufactured.
✅
100% Pure — No Fillers, Fully Traceable
Unlike Chinese or Vietnamese powder marketed as "ube," Vera Heritage products are traceable lot-by-lot, with third-party COA confirming zero maltodextrin or starch fillers.
